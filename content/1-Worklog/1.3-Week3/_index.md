### Week 3 Objectives:
* Deploy an application with automatic scaling and load balancing capabilities (Auto Scaling & ELB).
* Manage, control, and optimize AWS costs using AWS Budgets.
* Comprehensively monitor the system, resources, and applications through Amazon CloudWatch.

### Week 3 Achievements:
* **Scalable Architecture (Auto Scaling & ELB):** Successfully deployed a highly available, fault-tolerant architecture. The application automatically adjusts resources to maintain performance during traffic spikes while optimizing costs during periods of low demand.
* **Cost Control (AWS Budgets):** Mastered how to proactively monitor and control AWS costs. Successfully configured a real-time alert system when costs exceed thresholds, and clearly understood the flexibility of using Savings Plans instead of Reserved Instances for EC2.
* **System Monitoring (CloudWatch):** Gained the ability to comprehensively observe applications and infrastructure. Perfected skills in setting up Alarms to reduce mean time to resolution (MTTR) and creating custom Dashboards to monitor system health in real-time.
